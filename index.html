About Us

4TH GROUND is a film distribution company founded and run by Independent film entrepreneurs who are passionate about bridging the gap between independent filmmakers and international audiences. We understand the challenges that independent producers face when trying to access professional distribution channels, and we are committed to building a more open, accessible, and filmmaker-focused pathway into the streaming landscape.

Our mission is to help powerful stories connect with audiences worldwide through strategic digital distribution and professional delivery services.

Our Services

1. Pre-Sale Movie Store / TVOD

Filmmakers earn while we position films for major platforms. Filmmakers suggest their own rental and purchase prices.

2. Distribution Channels

SVOD, AVOD, TVOD, FAST Channels, inflight entertainment.

3. Technical Delivery

Encoding, QC, subtitles/CC, metadata, delivery packaging. To meet all platform specifications.

4. Marketing & Strategy

Positioning, trailers, artwork, promotional planning. We strengthen discoverability.

5. Inflight Entertainment

Airline systems for additional exposure beyond streaming.

6. Filmmaker-First Approach

Collaborative, hand-in-hand support from onboarding to release. Build lasting partnerships.
